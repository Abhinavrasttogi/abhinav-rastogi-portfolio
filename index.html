🤖 AI Orchestration · Enterprise · Europe · 9 Countries

9 countries, 15+ AI agents, zero classrooms — and a churn account that became 3×

A large enterprise operating across 9 European countries was quietly disengaging. Multilingual teams couldn't onboard at scale, adoption had flatlined, and the account was heading toward non-renewal. The fix wasn't more training. It was a fundamentally different strategy: an orchestrated layer of AI agents — 15+ voice agents speaking native languages across Europe, paired with learning orchestration agents that auto-assigned journeys, monitored progress, and triggered real-time interventions. No manual coordination. No classrooms. Just AI running the last mile of learning, at scale.

Signal

Account health scores declining across all regions. Onboarding completion near zero in non-English markets. Churn signal 90 days from renewal. Executive sponsor disengaged.

Orchestrate

Designed and deployed an AI agent orchestration strategy: 15+ voice agents in native European languages (Italian, French, German + more), learning orchestration agents auto-assigning role-based journeys, real-time progress monitoring, and AI-powered conversational assessments — all running without a single classroom session.

Expand

Completion rates recovered across all 9 countries. Leadership saw measurable ROI. The renewal conversation became an expansion conversation — new business units, new markets, same AI-orchestrated model. Churn risk became the portfolio's highest-value relationship.
